Patrik Winiger, PhD

Research Interest

I am interested in short-lived climate pollutants, their sources, effects on climate, health impacts, and potential mitigation pathways. This includes not only combustion products, but also other light absorbing aerosols and impurities in snow and ice; like algae, bacteria, dust, soot, or polymers. In short: macromolecules

Research projects

  • MACrAA - Macromolecular aerosols in the cryosphere from the Arctic to the Alps

Experience and Education

  • Project leader GIS modelling, Stadt Zürich, Air Quality Department, 2021-2022
  • Grant Advisor and Project Manager, ETH Zürich, EUGrantsAccess, 2018-2021
  • Postdoc, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, Earth and Climate unit, 2018-2019
  • PhD Student, Stockholm university, Department of Applied Sciences and Analytical Chemistry, 2011-2017
